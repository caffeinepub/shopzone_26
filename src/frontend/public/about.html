Our Story

Built by Engineers,
Driven by Impact

We started as a small team with big ideas. Today, NexaIT is a 40-person studio delivering world-class software to clients across 20+ countries.

Who We Are

A Team of Builders, Designers & Strategists

Founded in 2016, NexaIT was born from a simple belief: great software should be accessible to every business, not just the Fortune 500. We set out to build a company where technical excellence meets genuine partnership.

What started as three co-founders working from a garage has grown into a diverse, globally distributed team of engineers, designers, and consultants working across time zones to serve clients on every continent.

Our culture is built on transparency, continuous learning, and an obsessive focus on client outcomes — not just deliverables.
